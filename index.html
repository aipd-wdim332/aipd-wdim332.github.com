PDX Interactive

An Open Discussion on Breaking Into The Web Industry.

March 11th, 2014 @ 7PM

Art Institute of Portland
1122 NW Davis Street
Second Floor, Open Space

 
 

Panelists

Chris Bloom
Senior Developer
www.phase2technology.com

Chris is an avid design and development professional with a decade of experience under his belt. He's helped build and launch dozens of major websites including michaeljackson.com, ozzy.com and paulsimon.com. Chris eats Drupal and dreams Javascript working for Phase2Technology. Outside of work, he teaches at the Art Institute while co-leading the Portland Drupal Users Group. Chris obsessively collects development frameworks and tools that he swears he'll get to use "for real" some day. He holds an MBA from Washington State University - but don't hold it against him.

Brian Ferdinand
Developer/Designer
www.gravitatedesign.com

Brian is a Developer over at Gravitate Design and a recent graduate of The Art Institute of Portland. Brian hasdabbled in internet support, spent almost 7 years as a Master Certified mechanic for a reputable Mercedes Benz Dealership, but before all of that I had a curiousity for the web and wanted to learn how to build it. I built my first site early on, even before the existence of social sites. Since then he has continuosly followed the technology.

Miranda Slayter
UI Designer/Developer
www.slayterdesigns.co

Miranda started working in the interactive media field just a year into her degree at The Art Institute of Portland. Less then three years later and barley old enough to drink, she obtained her Bachelors of Computer Science. Already having numerous work experiences under her belt including mostly freelance but also several startups such as Night & Day Studios. She is constantly scouring the internet for the latest in web and mobile design and development. Currently working full time for a company out of California, ovacoast and as a contractor for a company here in Portland, Phoogoo.

Steven Loyer
Developer/Web Designer
www.StevenLoyer.com

Steven is currently finishing his last term at the Art Institute of Portland where he is obtaining his Bachelors degree in Web Design & Interactive Media.Steven has also just moved to a new job as the Lead Developer at Beacon Media in Anchorage, Alaska. Steven loves to work in Drupal, Sass and anything that "Scares" him. When he isn't working, Steven is found near Motocross/Supercross tracks and enjoying beautiful Alaska.
